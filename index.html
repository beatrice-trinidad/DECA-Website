RUTGERS DECA

We are an organization focused on preparing emerging leaders and entrepenuers by developing professional skills and achieving personal goals at Rutgers University.

About Us

DECA Inc. helps to prepare students to become leaders in business fields such as marketing, finance, consulting, and management at both high school and college levels. Now in 2015, Rutgers students in New Brunswick will have the opportunity to become a part of this student group that boasts a 70-year history. Rutgers University is now becoming one of the 275 collegiate chapters to participate in these events to better prepare students for the future.

We are DECA. R U?

DECA Members will...

Acquire 21st century skills

Competitive events of DECA support the development of critical thinking, problem solving, communication, collaboration, creativity, and innovation.

Learn to lead

DECA prioritizes learning effective leadership through goal setting, consensus building and project implementation. Aligned with 21st Century Skills, members will learn to work dynamically.

Gain valuable experience

Empowered through experience to become leaders, members will enhance their professional skills through real-world business simulations and case studies.

Network

Collaboration with businesses will give members the opportunity to meet and network with various business professionals throughout the year, as well as students on campus and across the nation.

Get Involved

The Rutgers University - New Brunswick chapter of DECA does not require you to be a business major or have formal experience. Anyone who is interested in developing their professional, social, and leadership skills are more than welcome to join!

Join more than 200,000 high school members in 5,000 classrooms in 3,500 schools across the world who are preparing for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality, management and entrepreneurship. You’ll have the opportunity to become an academically prepared, community oriented, professionally responsible, experienced leader through participation in DECA activities at the local, chartered association and international levels.

From community service projects, competitive events, educational conferences, leadership positions, networking and social media, you’ll attain the knowledge and skills you need to be college and career ready. As we prepare for next year's competition, we are looking for people who want to be a part of us for the 2014-2015 school year. If you're interested in joining the team or want to find out more about we do, feel free to contact us!

Meetings for the fall semester will be announced soon.
